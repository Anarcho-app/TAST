# Literature engagement (converge / diverge)

Short named engagement. Not a full historiography.

| Work | Point of contact | Converge | Diverge |
|------|------------------|----------|---------|
| **Hacker (2020)** and related census-based growth reconstructions | Administrative totals and implied natural increase | Value of archival census series as *data* | We refuse to treat corrected census paths as facts; `victors_reliability` can drive them to UNDEFINED |
| **SlaveVoyages / Eltis et al.** | Documented arrivals, voyage structure | Voyage databases are essential primary-linked infrastructure | Intra- and trans-Atlantic voyage counts do not license unmarked national path integrals when reliability → 0 |
| **Fogel & Engerman** | Cliometric profitability / efficiency debates | Micro-profitability can coexist with systemic costs | Demographic precision of owner-mediated series is not assumed from profitability arguments |
| **Gutman** | Family, culture, and critique of cliometric reduction | Family separation and cultural continuity matter | Qualitative strength does not substitute for a national head-count fact |
| **Steckel** | Anthropometric and selection-bias work | Selection and measurement bias are first-class | Those biases feed likelihood *uncertainty*, not automatic mechanism ranking |
| **Borucki & O’Malley (Intra-American database)** | Limits of voyage databases; contraband; quantitative work as question-generator | Their own limits language supports continuous reliability weighting | We formalize zero-weight collapse; they retain usable voyage estimates under trust |

## Genetics evidentiary bar (uniform)

Applied to **every** genetic / aDNA stream, for or against any hypothesis:

1. Historical aDNA sample sizes remain a tiny fraction of past populations; they support
   **presence and local lineage** claims, not national origin totals.
2. PCA / Fst / clustering results are interpreted under the same sample-size caution
   whether they appear to favor H1-like or H5-like readings.
3. Modern cohort / reference panels are not treated as direct samples of 17th–19th c. populations.
4. Physical remains (burial contexts) outrank unsupervised clustering when the two conflict
   on *presence*; neither alone ranks H1–H5 at r→0.

Violation of this bar in either direction is a bug, not a feature.

## Record sets with different producers (highest leverage for independence & discrimination)

| Record set | Producer & incentive | Why bias runs differently | TAST implication |
|------------|----------------------|---------------------------|------------------|
| Coastwise manifests (Steckel’s source) | Shipper, to prove domestic origin post-1808 | Incentive to prove *not* recently imported — opposite to census undercount pressure | Independent quantitative stream; can support or challenge H1 morphological predictions |
| Probate & appraisal (Berry, Pargas) | Estate courts; adversarial between heirs and creditors | Contested valuation → counts challenged *in the record itself* | Partial check on owner-mediated head-counts |
| Life insurance policies (Aetna, NY Life) | Actuaries pricing mortality risk | Commercially punished for error → systematic bias toward accurate mortality | Mortality signal with different producer than plantation ledgers |
| Church sacramental registers (Catholic LA/MD, Moravian, Anglican) | Clergy, not owners | Baptism/burial for sacramental reasons; indifferent to property accounting | Independent presence / family-structure signal |
| Freedmen’s Bureau marriage registers (Gutman’s source) | Bureau; self-reported | First large set where the population states its own family structure | Strengthens multi-generational continuity claims without owner mediation |
| **USCT pension files** | Sworn affidavits by formerly enslaved claimants and their communities | Testimony about pre-1865 facts, produced under oath, systematic, enormous | **Partial counterexample to absolute erasure claim**; makes silence precise (pre-1865 *and* self-authored-in-the-moment) rather than absolute |

**Irony worth recording**: Fogel’s own Early Indicators project assembled the Union Army / USCT dataset. The cliometrician treated as adversary in Stream 19 built the archive that most directly addresses the structural-silence problem.

## Theory allies (premise stated by historians)

Hartman, Fuentes, Smallwood, and Jennifer Morgan argue that the archive’s arithmetic is itself an artifact of the violence. That is the TAST premise stated in historical rather than parametric language. They converge on treating owner-mediated totals as conditional and on elevating structural silence and physical remains; they do not supply replacement national totals.

## Uniform genetics evidentiary bar (unchanged)

- aDNA / LAI / admixture series are physical-floor evidence (low coverage, high provenance quality).
- They constrain *presence* and broad ancestry components; they do **not** by themselves rank H1 vs H5 at national scale.
- No special pleading in either direction.

## Uniform genetics evidentiary bar (applied both ways)

- aDNA / LAI / admixture series are treated as physical-floor evidence (low coverage, high provenance quality).
- They constrain *presence* and broad ancestry components; they do **not** by themselves rank H1 vs H5 at national scale (coverage ~0.000017% or lower).
- No special pleading: the same bar that prevents “genetics proves H1” also prevents “genetics proves residual only”.
- See Streams 6, 17, 18, 24, 25 and physical_likelihoods.py.

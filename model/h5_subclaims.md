# H5 Operationalization — Falsifiable Sub-Claims (v5.7)

H5 (“mixed / undocumented mechanisms / residual uncertainty”) is **not** allowed to win only by being unfalsifiable.
It is decomposed into the following sub-claims. Each has a **rejection condition**.

These sub-claims inform interpretation of residual mass on H5; they do not automatically
override the quantitative stream product. They make H5 auditable.

---

## H5a — Genuine residual / incomplete records

**Claim:** After accounting for documented arrivals, classification change, and local natural
increase under known regimes, a material share of the demographic path remains under-determined
by surviving administrative series.

**Would be rejected if:**
- Independent physical + genealogical series tightly constrain the path (e.g. cumulative
  lower bounds from burial/aDNA/termination that match a specific H1–H4 path within
  stated error), **and**
- The residual between that path and administrative reconstructions is explained without
  leftover mechanism mass.

**Status:** Open — physical floor supports presence, not a full national path integral.

---

## H5b — Regional heterogeneity exceeds a single-mechanism story

**Claim:** Growth rates, sex ratios, or classification practices vary enough across regions
that no single mechanism (H1 or H4 alone) fits all major regions without large residuals.

**Would be rejected if:**
- Region-level series (where available) are statistically consistent with one dominant
  mechanism under pre-registered error bounds, **and**
- Outliers are few and explained by documented local shocks.

**Status:** Open — requires region-disaggregated primary tables (partially in raw/).

---

## H5c — Structural silence blocks decisive mechanism ranking

**Claim:** Anti-literacy laws, non-personhood, and owner-mediated quantification systematically
remove the observations that would discriminate H1–H4, so residual uncertainty is expected
rather than a modeling failure.

**Would be rejected if:**
- Pre-1865 enslaved-authored quantitative series exist at scale sufficient to rank mechanisms, **or**
- Independent non-administrative series (physical + genealogical) alone rank mechanisms
  without relying on owner/enumerator counts.

**Status:** Supported as a structural claim (floor-05, floor-06, Stream 27); does **not**
by itself assign high posterior to a specific demographic total.

---

## H5d — Symmetric overreach filter

**Claim:** Both “import-dominant only” and “local-increase only” narratives have been
over-extended beyond what primary evidence can carry; residual H5 mass reflects that symmetry.

**Would be rejected if:**
- One side produces a primary-linked path that survives zero-weight on administrative
  totals and out-predicts the other on physical/genealogical holdouts.

**Status:** Methodological stance — see also Stream 26 (meta overreach filter).

---

## Rule

H5 posterior mass in the engine is still produced by the quantitative stream product
(and prior). These sub-claims exist so that mass cannot be *interpreted* as “H5 wins
because it cannot lose.” Any report of high H5 should cite which of H5a–H5d is being claimed.
